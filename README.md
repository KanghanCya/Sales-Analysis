# Sales-Analysis

Business Problem

This project analyzes sales data to identify revenue patterns across time and customer segments, with the goal of supporting better business decisions.

Data Description

The dataset contains order-level information including order date; transcation amount, and customer identity.

Analysis

Data cleaning was performed to remove duplicates and handle missing values. Datetime features such as month and day of the week were extracted to enable time-based analysis.

Revenue was analyzed by month, day of the week, and customer.

Key Insights

February 2024 generated the highest total revenue.
Monday recorded the highest total sales among all days.
Customer C contributed the highest revenue, making them the most valuable customer.
Recommendations

Allocate more resources during high-performing periods such as Monday.
Develop retention strategies targeting high-value customers like Customer C.
